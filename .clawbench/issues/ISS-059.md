## Description
Dequeue-during-exit race — the 50ms heuristic used to check for remaining enqueued messages after session exit can miss messages that are enqueued during that window. The check is not synchronized with the enqueue path.

## Impact
Chat messages enqueued near session teardown are silently lost. Users may see incomplete conversation history.

## Suggestion
Replace the time-based heuristic with a synchronized signal (e.g., a channel or atomic flag) that the enqueue side sets and the dequeue side checks before finalizing. Ensure the dequeue loop drains all pending messages under the lock before marking the queue as closed.
