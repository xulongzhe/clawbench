## Description
Limited mode off-by-one — cancelled or crashed runs increment `run_count`, so tasks complete after fewer successful runs than configured. For example, a task with `limit: 3` that has 2 successes and 1 crash will reach `run_count == 3` and stop, having only completed 2 successful runs.

## Impact
Scheduled tasks stop before completing the intended number of successful executions. Users may not get the full expected output from limited-run tasks.

## Suggestion
Decrement `run_count` on cancelled/crashed executions, or track `success_count` separately from `run_count`. Only increment `run_count` on successful completion. Adjust the completion check to compare `success_count` against the limit.
